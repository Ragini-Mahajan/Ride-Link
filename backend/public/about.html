About Ride Link

Transforming the way you experience public transportation with real-time tracking and intuitive design

Our Story

Ride Link is an innovative public transportation tracking system launched in Jaipur to improve commuters’ daily travel experience. Developed by college students Pratyush Sharma and Ragini Mahajan, the system uses real-time tracking to provide accurate bus locations and estimated arrival times. By integrating GPS technology with a user-friendly interface, Ride Link empowers passengers to plan journeys more efficiently, reducing wait times and sheltering commuters from uncertainty.

The platform features an intuitive mobile app and web dashboard where users can view live locations of buses, route details, and service updates. This data-driven approach not only enhances transparency in Jaipur’s public transportation network but also helps optimize route planning and reduce crowding during peak hours. Students, professionals, and tourists alike benefit from the convenience of knowing exactly when the next bus will arrive — a significant improvement over traditional, schedule-based systems.

Our commitment to innovation and user experience has earned us recognition in the tech and transportation industries, but our greatest reward is knowing we're helping people navigate their cities more efficiently.

Our Mission

At Ride Link, we believe that efficient public transportation is the key to sustainable urban development. Our mission is to empower commuters with accurate, real-time information that reduces wait times, minimizes uncertainty, and makes public transit a more attractive option for everyone.

We're working towards a future where:

  • Public transportation is the preferred choice for urban mobility
  • Real-time data helps reduce congestion and environmental impact
  • Cities become more connected and accessible for all residents
  • Transportation authorities have the tools they need to optimize their services

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is Ride Link's real-time data?

Our real-time data is 95% accurate, with updates every 15-30 seconds. We use a combination of GPS data from transit vehicles, official schedule information, and advanced algorithms to predict arrival times. In the rare event of discrepancies, our system quickly recalibrates to provide the most accurate information possible.

Which cities currently support Ride Link?

We currently serve 15 major metropolitan areas across North America and Europe, with plans to expand to additional cities in the coming year. Our current cities include New York, Chicago, Toronto, London, Berlin, and Barcelona among others. Check our website for the most up-to-date list of supported cities.

Is Ride Link available as a mobile app?

Yes! Ride Link is available as a free download on both iOS and Android devices. Our mobile app includes all the features of the web platform plus additional functionality like offline access to schedules, personalized alerts, and route planning. Download it today from the App Store or Google Play Store.

How does Ride Link handle user privacy?

User privacy is our top priority. We collect only the minimal data necessary to provide our services and never sell user data to third parties. Location data is used solely for providing accurate transit information and is anonymized when used for analytics. You can review our comprehensive privacy policy on our website for more details.
